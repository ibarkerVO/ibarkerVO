A Little About Me

I'm a free-wheeling dreamer who's always liked the idea of plotting my own path towards success.

My voice is the auditory equivalent of smoke. It is deep, sinuous, winding and subtle, yet warm. Some voices are bold, in your face and explosive--they demand your attention. My voice, however, whispers at the fringes of perception and draws you in to listen. It persuades without pushiness and evokes contemplative thought. A touch hyperbolic? Perhaps. But listen to some of my work and decide for yourself.

Although social media is often a powerful tool, it can also be a hindrance to deep work--the kind of work that brings magical results that only come from deep, attentive devotion to one's craft. Social media inconveniences me by pulling me away from my primary responsibility of providing excellent voice over work, and as a result I do not utilize it outside of LinkedIn. My apologies in advance.
